My Sewing History

I was taught how to hand sew at a young age, but only became serious about sewing early 2023. I don't have that much expierence but I've made great progress. We had a sewing machine sitting in our garage so I decided to make good use out of it. My first project was a bucket hat. Then I wanted to try something simple and tried making a tank top. That ended up being my most difficult project because of my bad fabric choice and my old cheap sewing machine. I moved on to making a milkmaid style top. It became difficult because my sewing machine wasn't wokring properly. I decided to buy a new sewing machine which has been working wonderfully. Then I finished my milkmaid top. After that I bought some patterns and started making a dress. I worked slowly but regularly on the dress. It turned out amazing. I also finished that difficult tanktop with the help of my new sewing machine.
